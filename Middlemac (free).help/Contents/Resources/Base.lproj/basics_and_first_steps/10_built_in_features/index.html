Built-in features

Explore some of the many built-in features that Middlemac brings to you.

Templates
Middlemac separates your content from the overall visual appearance of your helpbook via the use of templates.
CSS and image_widths.css
Have a brief look at the included CSS so that you can decide how to best use it or easily change it to suit your own needs.
Breadcrumbs
Breadcrumbs help prevent your users from becoming lost, especially if you have deeply nested content. Get to know your options here.
Navigator
Learn how the page navigator can make navigation easy for your users, as well as how to turn it on or off for groups and individual pages.
markdown_links and markdown_images
Every time you start the server or build your helpbook, Middlemac builds these two helpful files for you. Learn to take advantage of them.
Resources overview
Learn how to work with Middleman resources and the extensions that Middlemac brings to them.
Helpers overview
A brief look at how Middlemac’s Helpers can help you out.
Partials overview
Take advantage of Middlemac’s partials, and maybe even learn to write your own!
